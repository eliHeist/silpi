Who are we?

At Sil-Pi, we are dedicated to providing a diverse range of services tailored to meet your unique needs.
With a commitment to excellence and a customer-centric approach, we strive to make a positive impact in various facets of your life.
With a vision to contribute positively to the communities we serve, Sil-Pi is more than just a business, it's a partner in your journey towards success and fulfillment.

Our Mission:

To provide accessible, reliable, and ethical services that enhance the well-being and prosperity of our customers and communities.

Company Objectives

  1. To carry on all or any of the professions or business of social and other kinds of research, urban and rural planners, highway, traffics, airport and other transport planners, health planners, and general planners, designers, tracers, blue printers, copyists and investigators by way of operations research.
  2. To carry on the business of agricultural farming, including but not limited to the establishment, buying or otherwise acquiring farms and properties thereon or useful thereto; and carry on the business of buying, selling or otherwise disposing of agricultural produce of any kind, type or description.
  3. To establish research and development centres for the business of the Company.
  4. To carry on all or any of the trades and business of livestock and crop farmers, breeders or dealers in livestock, crop and all agricultural products, to import and export the same and the business of gardeners, agriculturalists and dairymen and any other trade or business in connection with agriculture.
  5. To carry on the business of manufacturers’ and purchasers’ representatives, importers and exporters and or dealers in agricultural and other goods and services.
  6. To set up model farms of crops and animals, to supply improved seeds, seedlings and animals to farmers and entities within and outside Uganda.
  7. To carry on the business as manufacturers, producers, processors, growers, distillers, refiners, makers, inventors, convectors, importers, exporters, traders, buyers, sellers, retailers, wholesalers, suppliers, indenters, bottlers, packers, movers, preservers, stockists, agents, sub-agents, merchants, distributors, consignors, jobbers, brokers, concessionaires or otherwise deal in all kinds of products including but not limited crop and animal products, pesticides, insecticides, fungicides and all types of chemicals used apiculture, pisciculture, sericulture, horticulture, poultry farming and animal husbandry.
  8. To carry on the business of money changers and dealers of foreign currencies and to buy, sell and deal in foreign currencies of all descriptions and to convert foreign currencies into Uganda shillings and vice versa.
  9. To carry on the business of providing all kinds of services relating to Information Technology (IT) including but not limited to the import, export, supply, assemble, manufacture, sale and purchase of computers hardware and software, system peripherals and other electrical and electronic equipment, computers and computer related equipment namely computer applications and programming services, operating and managing all computer hardware and software, system support and maintenance, data preparation, research and survey on all computers related problems, administrative services, procurement and supply services, technical, scientific and operational advice including material and instructional aids, management coordination, supervision and control services, exhibition, seminars and computer personnel and management training courses and other ancillary services relating to IT and the computer industry.
  10. To establish and carry on the business of buying and selling new and used motor cycles, vehicles, caravans, trailers, petrol oil and petro products, spare parts, accessories, radios, cassette players, rubber and electronic goods, gifts, books and confectionery.
  11. To lend to or grant or provide credit or financial assistance to any person or company in any case in which such grant or provision is considered likely or indirectly to further any of the objects of the company or interests of its members.
  12. To provide, offer, hire, engage, develop, improve any consultancy services otherwise deal in any kind of events like social, cultural, artistic, amusement to the corporate bodies, firms, clubs, associations, individuals and all other legal entities either in Uganda (or) abroad.
